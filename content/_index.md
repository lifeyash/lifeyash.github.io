Hi, I'm Yash

I spend time trying to understand how computers work beneath the layers built on top of them.

Most of what you'll find here are notes, experiments, half-finished ideas, and things I wanted to remember later. Sometimes they work. Sometimes they become reminders of what not to do.

I like building from simple pieces and understanding why they work before moving on.

Lately this has mostly meant working closer to the machine — ARM Cortex-M internals, registers, exceptions, context switching, debugging, and understanding software by observing what actually happens underneath.
